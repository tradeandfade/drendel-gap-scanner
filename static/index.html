Create a Free Alpaca Account

Alpaca is a free stock market data provider. You'll need an account to power the scanner's real-time data.

1. Go to alpaca.markets
2. Click "Sign Up" and create an account
3. Choose "Paper Trading" (free, no real money needed)
4. Complete the sign-up process and verify your email
2

Generate Your API Keys

API keys let the scanner access market data through your Alpaca account.

1. Log in to your Alpaca dashboard
2. On the left sidebar, click "Paper Trading"
3. Find the "API Keys" section and click "Generate" (or "Regenerate")
4. You'll see two values:
  • API Key ID — starts with "PK"
  • Secret Key — a long string (only shown once!)
5. Copy both keys and save them somewhere safe

Important: The Secret Key is only shown once. If you lose it, you'll need to regenerate both keys.

Build Your Watchlist

After your keys are validated, go to the Settings tab to add the stock symbols you want to scan.

• Type a symbol and press Enter, or paste a list of symbols
• The scanner will fetch historical data and calculate all gap zones for each symbol
• This may take a minute for large watchlists (50+ symbols)
5

Configure Your Alerts

In the Settings tab, you can adjust:

• Zone Proximity — Set to 0% to only alert when price enters a gap zone, or increase to get early warnings when price approaches
• Refresh Interval — How often the scanner checks prices (15 seconds to 5 minutes)
• Moving Averages — Customize colors and choose SMA or EMA for each period
6

Understanding the Scanner

The scanner monitors your watchlist and alerts you when price enters gap zones:

Support Gaps (Green) — Price pulling back into a gap-up zone. Potential bounce area.
Resistance Gaps (Red) — Price pushing up into a gap-down zone. Potential reversal area.
Untested Gaps (Yellow) — New gaps that haven't been tested yet.

• Click any alert card to open a full chart with moving averages and zone overlays
• The alert feed sidebar shows alerts chronologically as they fire throughout the day
• Alerts reset automatically before each new trading day (9:30 AM ET)
• Each zone only triggers one alert per day — no spam
