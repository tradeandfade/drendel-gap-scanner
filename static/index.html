Generate Your API Keys

1. Log in to your Alpaca dashboard
2. Click "Paper Trading" in the left sidebar
3. Find "API Keys" and click "Generate"
4. Copy both values:
  • API Key ID — starts with "PK"
  • Secret Key — shown once only!

Save your Secret Key immediately — it won't be shown again.

Configure & Scan

Zone Proximity — 0% = alert on zone entry. Increase for early warnings.
Refresh Interval — How often prices are checked (15s to 5min).

The scanner runs during market hours (9:30 AM - 4:00 PM ET). Alerts reset each morning before market open.
6

Understanding Alerts

Support (Green) — Price pulling back into a gap-up zone.
Resistance (Red) — Price pushing up into a gap-down zone.
Untested (Yellow) — New gaps, first contact.

Click any card to open a chart with MAs and zone overlays. Each zone only fires one alert per day.
